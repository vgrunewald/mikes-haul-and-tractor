Stump Removal

Large stump prior to removal

Stumps are probably the most difficult naturally occurring yard ornament for homeowners to remove. Grinding them is becoming increasingly dangerous as pieces of the stump can be flung into neighbors yards.

By pulling out the stump using an earthmover, I can remove the stump while minimizing the danger to surrounding structures.


For a free estimate on a stump removal, just call Mike's or send a message online.
Removing stumps using an earthmover is much safer than most
do-it-yourself methods.

Stump Pulling and Haul Away

After pulling out a stump, I'll load it into the truck and haul it away. From start to finish, Mike's makes removing stumps on your property fast and easy.
