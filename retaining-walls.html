Retaining Walls

Due to our hilly terrain, retaining walls are an integral part of landscaping in the Northwest. I have built many different types of retaining walls and am more than happy to share my knowledge of materials and past experience. Pictured at right is one of the retaining walls I have built.


Why Build a Retaining Wall?

There are many reasons for homeowners to add retaining walls to their landscape.

For example, if you have a steep hill on your property that makes access or planting difficult, we can divide it into flat "steps" separated by retaining walls. I also offer grading and levelling services to accomplish this.

Or, you may wish to create a sectioned garden with a unique terraced layout. Whatever the reason, if you can envision it, I can help you build it!
