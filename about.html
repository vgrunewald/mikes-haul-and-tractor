About Mike's Hauling and Tractor Work

Mike McArthur, Owner of Mike's Hauling & Tractor

Having lived on the Sammamish Plateau for more than thirty years, I started this business in 1997 when I retired from teaching on Mercer Island.

Since I have worked with tractors on a casual basis most of my life and have always enjoyed helping others, the origination of Mike's Hauling and Tractor Work seemed like a natural.

I can help you clean, modify, or maintain yards and acreages. When you call, I try to come out within a day to discuss your project and clarify how I can meet your needs.
